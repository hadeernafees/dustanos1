About our company

DUSTANOS is a car manufacturing company based in Khobar, Saudi Arabia, that is quickly becoming a household name in the automotive industry. Founded in 2018, the company has been making waves in the Middle East market with its innovative designs and cutting-edge technology. DUSTANOS has a vision to revolutionize the automobile industry, and they’re doing so by producing high-quality cars with exceptional performance. The company prides itself on being environmentally friendly, with a goal to produce cars that are both sustainable and efficient. This is evident in their use of advanced materials and technologies in the manufacturing process, resulting in cars that are lighter, more fuel-efficient, and emit less carbon dioxide. DUSTANOS believes that this approach is the way forward, and they are committed to making a positive impact on the environment. DUSTANOS is also committed to providing its customers with exceptional service. The company has a state-of-the-art facility in Khobar, where they offer a range of services, including maintenance, repairs, and customization. They also have a team of highly trained technicians who are dedicated to ensuring that every customer receives the best care and attention. In addition to their commitment to sustainability and customer service, DUSTANOS is also known for their sleek and stylish designs. Their cars are not only functional, but they also look great on the road. The company’s attention to detail in the design process is evident in every aspect of their cars, from the exterior to the interior. Overall, DUSTANOS is a company that is making waves in the automobile industry. Their commitment to sustainability, customer service, and innovative design is setting them apart from the competition. With a bright future ahead, DUSTANOS is a company that is definitely worth watching.
